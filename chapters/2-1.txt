6 advantages of cloud
	- trade capital expense for variable expense
	- benefit from massive economies of scale
	- stop guessing about capacity
	- increase speed and agility
	- stop spending money running data centers
	- go global quickly

3 types of cloud computing:
	- iaas - infrastructure as a service (EC2)
	- paas - platform as a service (elastic-beanstalk, godaddy)
	- saas - software as a service (gmail)

3 Types of cloud computing deployments:
	- public cloud - aws, azure, gcp
	- hybrid - muxture of public and private
	- private (on prem) - you manage it, in your datacenter, opstack or vmware
